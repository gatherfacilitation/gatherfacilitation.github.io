Facilitator Extraordinaire dedicated to helping individuals & groups explore inner landscapes, transform conflict, and heal & create collectively.

Approach

My approach is informed by a rich mixture of my studies in somatic psychotherapy and Processwork facilitation, my training & learning in social justice and anti-oppression, and my personal explorations through women's rites of passage work, travel and my Jewish lineage. I dance equally with the sacred and the profane, at once holding space for deep transformation while infusing my work with humor and play.
