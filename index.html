About

Elena Pinsky works at the intersections of creativity and connection. A trained facilitator, she is excited to help groups transform conflict and explore collective healing. She also works 1:1 with individuals to help them align more deeply with their life paths and purposes.

To her career in interpersonal work, she brings five years of experience in operations management, deep empathy and compassion, and a keen understanding of the ways in which systems of oppression and inequitable distribution of power impact people at both interpersonal and institutional levels.

With an eye for beauty, Elena is never without a creative project, be it curating aesthetically-pleasing spaces, cooking, preserving, macrame-ing, screen-printing, watercoloring, woodworking, laser-cutting, face-painting, songwriting or planning a dinner party.
